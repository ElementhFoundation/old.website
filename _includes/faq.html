FAQ

  • How to contribute to Private Pre-Sale?

    1. Go to https://elementh.io/;
    2. Click "PRE-SALE" button;
    3. Click "Join Whitelist" button;
    4. Choose "Private Pre-Sale" and fill the form and click "JOIN" button, then click "SUCCESS" button (you are in whitelist now).
    5. At https://elementh.io/presale.html enter your wallet address and click "CONTRIBUTE".
    6. Copy our smart-contract address by clicking "Copy Address" button (or BTC address if you choose to invest by BTC).
    7. Send not less than 10 ETH or 1 BTC to that address.

  • How to contribute to Pre-ICO or ICO?

    1. Go to https://elementh.io/;
    2. Click "PRE-SALE" button;
    3. Click "Join Whitelist" button;
    4. Choose "Pre-ICO" or "ICO" and fill the form and click "JOIN" button, then click "SUCCESS" button (you are in whitelist now).
    5. Wait for your round to contribute.

  • Where are you incorporated?

    Our current business is in Russia and our Foundation will be incorporated in Switzerland or Gibraltar.

  • What blockchain do you use?

    For ICO we use Ethereum blockchain, but after ICO we'll develop our own blockchain Elementh and change ERC-20 tokens to Elementh tokens in Elementh blockchain.

  • Are your organization is profit or non-profit?

    Elementh Foundation is non-profit organization.

  • How will your tokens be used?

    They will be used for all operations in Elementh blockchain (like ETH in Ethereum blockchain and EOS in EOS blockchain).

  • Do you have bounty or referral program?

    We'll have bounty and referral programs after Pre-ICO stage. Actually we can pay referral fee if you have good channels or direct investors.
